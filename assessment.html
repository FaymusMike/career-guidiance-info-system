RIASEC Career Assessment

This assessment is based on the Holland Codes (RIASEC) theory, which categorizes people and careers into six types:

R Realistic

Practical, hands-on, technical

I Investigative

Analytical, intellectual, scientific

A Artistic

Creative, expressive, original

S Social

Helping, teaching, caring

E Enterprising

Leadership, persuasive, managerial

C Conventional

Organized, detail-oriented, systematic

How it works:
  • You will answer 30 questions about your interests and preferences
  • Answer honestly based on what you enjoy, not what you think you should
  • There are no right or wrong answers
  • Your answers will be used to match you with suitable careers
